Our Services

EMERGENCY

There is always an ambulance around you and we have created a platform that connect you with the nearest ambulance in the event of emergency.

G1 uses your precise location and live traffic updates to locate and dispatch the nearest ambulance to your location.

What all does this app do during an emergency?

  • Ambulance Routing
  • Notifies your emergency contacts
  • Sends your basic information to the hospital

    • G1 shares your basic information, insurance details and basic medical information with the hospital in the event of emergency and has proved to be a game changer.
    G1 also enables you to request an ambulance for someone else in the event of an emergency, so that we can provide critical care for everyone.

    PLANNED RIDES

    Scheduling an ambulance simplified. Select the type of ambulance, date, time and locations and we would provide you with the quality ambulance services at a very effective competitive cost. To know more download G1 Healthapp.

    VIRTUAL BLOOD BANK

    Once you signup you can furnish your basic details along with your blood group. If you wish to be a donor you can consent for the same and whenever there is need of your blood group around your selected locations (Home, office etc) you would be notified. If you are available you can walk into the hospital where the blood is needed and donate.

    Once you donate blood you will be put into hibernation mode (for blood donation) for next 3 months and post that time period you would be notified again for new requirements.

    Why should I become an on demand (Virtual) Donor? Do you know how much blood is wasted every year?
